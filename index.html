Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my notes saved?

Your notes are saved locally in your browser's storage. They never leave your device and are not sent to any server. This keeps your data private and lets you work offline.

Can I export my notes?

You can copy the content of your notes and paste it elsewhere. For full export features, check back soon ‚Äì we're always improving!

What if I clear my browser data?

Clearing browser data (cache, cookies) will remove your notes. To keep them safe, regularly copy important notes or back them up manually.

Can I access my notes on another device?

Currently, notes are stored locally per device. We're exploring sync options. For now, you can copy/paste your notes to share them across devices.

Is StickyDraft free?

Yes! StickyDraft is completely free to use. No ads, no subscriptions, no strings attached. We believe in simple, open tools for creative minds.
